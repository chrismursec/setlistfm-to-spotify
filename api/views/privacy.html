Privacy Policy

SetlistFM to Spotify is an open-source project that allows users to import setlists from setlist.fm to their Spotify account. We do not store any user data on our servers. The only data we store is the user's Spotify access token in their browser's local storage. This token is used to make requests to the Spotify API on behalf of the user. We do not have access to the user's Spotify account or any other personal information.

You can revoke access to this app at any time by logging into your Spotify account and visiting your apps page.
